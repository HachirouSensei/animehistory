History of Anime.

Anime is a distinctive style of animation originating in Japan. The feel of anime is very similar to manga, which refers to comic book art. Anime can be either television episodes or full length movies. In Japan, any form of animation is considered anime. However, in Western culture, anime refers to the specific Japanese cartoon style of artwork.
